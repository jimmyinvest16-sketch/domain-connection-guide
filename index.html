Method 1: Domain Transfer

Prerequisites

1

Unlock Your Domain

Contact your current registrar to unlock your domain for transfer

2

Get Authorization Code

Obtain the auth code (EPP code) from your current registrar

3

Verify Contact Info

Ensure your domain's contact information is up-to-date

Important Restrictions

Note: Domains cannot be transferred within 60 days of:

  • New registration
  • Previous transfer
  • Contact information changes (if 60-day lock applied)

Transfer Process

1

Visit GoDaddy Transfers Page

Go to GoDaddy's domain transfer page and search for your domain

2

Verify Domain Status

Confirm your domain shows as unlocked (green status bar)

3

Enter Authorization Code

Input the auth code from your current registrar

4

Complete Checkout

Choose domain protection options and finalize the transfer

DNS Connection Process

1

Access Domain Registrar

Log in to your current domain registrar's control panel

2

Find DNS Management

Navigate to DNS settings or nameserver management

3

Switch to Custom Nameservers

Change from default to custom nameserver settings

4

Enter GoDaddy Nameservers

Input the NS1 and NS2 addresses provided by GoDaddy

5

Save and Verify

Save changes and wait 24-48 hours for propagation

Common DNS Record Types

A Record

Connects your domain to an IP address for website hosting

CNAME

Points to another domain or URL (cannot point to IP address)

MX Record

Manages email routing and ensures messages reach your inbox

TXT Record

Verifies domain ownership and sets up email sender policies

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Website Not Loading After Changes

  • Wait up to 48 hours for DNS propagation
  • Clear your browser cache
  • Use DNS lookup tools to verify settings

Can't Find Authorization Code

  • Check your email from the registrar
  • Look in your registrar's control panel
  • Contact your current registrar for assistance

Transfer Taking Too Long

  • Check email for approval requests
  • Verify authorization code is correct
  • Contact GoDaddy support for status update
